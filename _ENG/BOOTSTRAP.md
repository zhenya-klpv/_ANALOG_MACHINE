## Phases (bootstrap)

### Phase 1 — Portfolio (0–3 mo) ← **NOW**

- No workshop lease, no debt, no large purchases
- One beautiful part on E36 (hood — first candidate)
- Full process content
- Inspection + baseline — free (car already exists)

**Cash out:** materials for one part only — minimize, buy as needed.

### Phase 2 — First paid work (3–6 mo)

- Small orders only
- **Materials + time deposit — paid by client before start**
- Don't buy materials on own money

### Phase 3 — Pre-order / deposit (6–12 mo)

- If demand exists for parts — **50–70% deposit** before work
- Standardize 2–3 products (hood, splitter, door card)

### Phase 4 — Workshop

- Only when **rent is paid by work**, not hope
- Or partner / investor with capital

### Phase 5+ — Long-term vision

Full packages, AM36 builds, commissions — see [`PRICING.md`](PRICING.md), [`VISION.md`](VISION.md).  
**Only after** stable cash flow and proof.

## Financial rules

1. **No materials on credit card** without confirmed paid order
2. **50–70% deposit** before any client work
3. **Don't scale burn** (rent, tools, hires) until repeatable orders
4. **Count hours** — composite "we'll do it ourselves" = 80+ hrs easily
5. **Personal runway** — loans and rent take priority over project
