# Content & Video — Analog Machine

> **Content isn't an add-on. Content is part of the product.**

You sell not only cars and parts. You sell **dream, trust, taste, process, and sense of level**. Without good content that's nearly impossible.

Great work + bad shooting = "another garage project."  
Same work + beautiful presentation = **brand**.

---

## 5 content jobs

Every reel / post should cover at least one job. Hero content — all five.

### 1. Show quality

Level visible through screen:

- gaps, surfaces, paint in sun
- carbon, fasteners, backside finish
- interior, wiring, how panels sit
- underside, suspension in action

Not "here's the car" — **"level visible even through screen."**

### 2. Build trust

- explain decisions ("why this part")
- before/after
- honest about difficulties
- document stages, hours, spec sheets

### 3. Create desire

Atmosphere, not just spec:

- early morning Skyline / Highway 9, fog
- cold start, door sound, engine
- hand on wheel, leather / Alcantara texture
- carbon in side light, bench details
- canyon drive-through

### 4. Raise perceived value

Without content: "Why $15k / $120k?"  
With content: "I see where the money went. I see the level."

Weight shots, process hours, material close-ups — **justify the price**.

### 5. Form the brand

Recognizable feel: shooting style, light, edit, tone of voice.

> "Ah, Analog Machine — always beautiful, quiet, clean, engineering, premium."

Style: [`MEDIA_STYLE.md`](MEDIA_STYLE.md) — **quiet premium engineering**.

## Production essentials

### Light
Soft key 45° · fill · rim · not only fluorescent ceiling.

### Frame cleanliness
Remove clutter; tools as props; workshop shot graphically.

### Sound
Lav + recorder; ASMR process; motor/road; minimal "epic trailer" music.

### Consistency
One LUT · one font · one cover system · one edit pace.

### Kit (start)
LED panels + diffusion · tripod · gimbal · lav mic · reflector.
