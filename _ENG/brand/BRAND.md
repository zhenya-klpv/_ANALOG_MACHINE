# Brand — Analog Machine

> **Brand core:** Analog Machine · **Handbuilt in California**

Three meanings in one chain:

| Word | Meaning |
|-------|----------|
| **Analog** | Not digital appliance — mechanical object |
| **Machine** | Real car, not gadget |
| **Handbuilt in California** | Craft, place, culture — Highway 9, Skyline, Pacific Coast |

> We build **analog cars by hand in California**.

Not "we do BMW." Not "we're better than BMW" — **our interpretation of the platform**.

**Don't use:** garage, customs, tuning, performance shop, **built by hands** (grammar).

**On the car:**
- ✅ *Handbuilt in California*
- ✅ *Built by hand in California*
- ❌ *built by hands*

*Handbuilt* — Porsche / Singer / coachbuilt culture. Shorter, more premium — perfect for a plaque.

## Phrasing — comparison

| Phrase | Usage |
|-------|---------------|
| **Handbuilt in California** | **Favorite** — plaque, badge, short signature |
| Built by hand in California | Long form, site, narrative |
| Hand-built in California | Formal; slightly less clean visually |
| Crafted by hand in California | Luxury; can sound jewelry/furniture |
| Analog machines, built by hand in California. | Ready phrase for site / hero |

---

## Rolex vs Apple Watch

Not "old is better than new." **An analog thing can be more expensive, more desirable, more emotional than a digital one.**

Rolex doesn't update over Wi-Fi — but it's desirable: mechanism, status, object.  
Same for **Analog Machine** — feel, presence, motion — not feeds.
