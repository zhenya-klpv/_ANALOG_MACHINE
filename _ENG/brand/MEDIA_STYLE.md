# Media Style — Quiet Premium Engineering

> Visual and tonal language of Analog Machine.  
> Not loud, not shouty, not TikTok — **quiet confidence**.

**Cultural reference:** **Phoebe Philo** — Old Céline (2008–2017), same restraint in image. See [`BRAND.md`](BRAND.md#brand-dna--phoebe-philo).

---

## Celine test (before publish)

Ask before every post, reel, or photo set:

| Question | Pass | Fail |
|----------|------|------|
| Would this fit a Phoebe Philo lookbook? | Quiet, material-led, architectural | Loud, logo-first, chaotic |
| Is the frame breathing? | Space, light, one subject | Clutter, filters, stickers |
| Does it whisper or shout? | Whisper | Shout |
| Logo / badge visible? | Small, earned | Dominant, promotional |
| Color grade? | Neutral, stone, cream, black | Oversaturated, neon, chrome sparkle |

If it fails → re-shoot or re-edit. **Better one quiet frame than ten loud ones.**

> Quiet, clean, engineering, premium — **Handbuilt in California**.

**End card:** ANALOG MACHINE · Handbuilt in California

Viewer watches a reel and understands: *"Ah, this is Analog Machine — they always look like this."*

## Visual language

- **Light** — decides everything; carbon, metal, leather without good light = garage
- **Frame cleanliness** — even ordinary workshop shot graphically, no clutter
- **Textures** — weave, stitch, machined edge, paint in sun
- **Space** — air in frame, not clutter
- **Movement** — slow push-in, slow motion details, rolling shots
- **Place** — morning, fog, Skyline, Highway 9, metal on bench

---

## Sound (underrated luxury layer)

- Ratchet click, sanding, vacuum pump
- Door close, hood close
- Cold start, idle, pull
- Workshop footsteps, Alcantara rustle
- Road ambience — tires, wind, downshift
- Minimal music; ambient or silence

## Brand consistency

One style for everything:

| Element | Rule |
|---------|---------|
| Color grade | One LUT / preset for whole project |
| Edit pace | Slow–medium; no jump-scare cuts |
| Titles | Minimal; one font |
| YouTube covers | One system (layout + type) |
| Instagram grid | Alternating: hero / detail / process |
| Logo | End of reel, quiet |

---

## Rubrics (repeatable formats)

| Rubric | Format | Goal |
|---------|--------|------|
| **Before / After** | Carousel, 30 sec reel | Quality, trust |
| **Why this part** | 60–90 sec talking + b-roll | Engineering, expertise |
| **On the bench** | Detail stills + macro video | Materials, desire |
| **Build diary** | 5–12 min YouTube | Process, trust |
| **Canyon morning** | Cinematic reel | Desire, brand |
| **Weight & spec** | Static + number overlay | Value ($ justified) |
| **Founder note** | 1–2 min, direct camera | Philosophy, who we are |

---

## What content sells

1. **The car** — "Here's what we can build"
2. **Parts** — "Here's what you can buy now"
3. **Workshop / approach** — "Here's why to trust us with your project"

Every post should explicitly or implicitly lead to one of three.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Dirty service bay in frame without composition
- Only wide shots without detail proof
- Only hero without process (no trust)
- Only process without hero (no desire)
- Different style reel to reel
- One beautiful reel every 4 months instead of rhythm
