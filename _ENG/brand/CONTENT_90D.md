# Content Strategy — 90 Days (e36-00)

> Practical plan for first 3 months.  
> **Bootstrap mode:** focus on **one part** (not hood — see [`SPRINT_90D.md`](SPRINT_90D.md)), not full build — see [`BOOTSTRAP.md`](BOOTSTRAP.md).  
> Goal: **rhythm + style + trust** → first paid inquiry.

Related: [`CONTENT.md`](CONTENT.md) · [`MEDIA_STYLE.md`](MEDIA_STYLE.md) · [`builds/e36-00/content/CONTENT_LOG.md`](builds/e36-00/content/CONTENT_LOG.md)

---

## Rhythm (keep steady)

| Channel | Frequency |
|-------|---------|
| Stories | 3–4× / week |
| Reels | 2× / week |
| Feed post (carousel / still) | 1× / week |
| YouTube | 1× / 2–4 weeks |
| Hero film | 1× per project finale |

> Better steady in one style than one masterpiece per quarter.

## Month 1 — Foundation & Baseline

**Theme:** "We're starting. Here's where from."

### Week 1–2: Donor story
| Deliverable | Type | Channel |
|-------------|-----|-------|
| Cinematic walkaround (as-is) | Hero b-roll | Archive, teaser later |
| Inspection highlights | Reel 30–45 sec | IG |
| «Why this E36» founder clip | 60–90 sec | IG + YT Short |
| Baseline carousel (8–10 frames) | Before | IG feed |
| Mood board + LUT lock | Internal | Style bible |

### Week 3–4: Philosophy & setup
| Deliverable | Type | Channel |
|-------------|-----|-------|
| «What we don't build» | Carousel / reel | IG |
| Workshop tour (clean angles) | Reel | IG |
| Build brief reveal (1 page visual) | Carousel | IG |
| YouTube Ep.1: **Project start + inspection** | 5–8 min | YouTube |

**Month 1 KPI:** 8 reels, 4 posts, 4 story weeks, 1 YT episode, style locked.

## 5 content jobs — monthly checklist

| Job | M1 | M2 | M3 |
|--------|----|----|-----|
| 1. Quality (details in frame) | baseline | composites | fitment |
| 2. Trust (process, honesty) | inspection | layup hours | engineering choices |
| 3. Desire (atmosphere) | founder story | craft ASMR | materials / sound |
| 4. Value (where money goes) | build scope | weight/spec | package hints |
| 5. Brand (recognizable style) | LUT lock | consistent grid | tone established |
