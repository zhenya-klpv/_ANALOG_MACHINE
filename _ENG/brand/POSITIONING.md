# Positioning — California Canyon BMW

> Not "BMW restomod" — too broad.  
> **California canyon car** — lightweight analog car for mountain roads.

---

## Niche

**Analog Machine** — **Handbuilt in California**. AM36 for Highway 9, Skyline, Pacific Coast — not "BMW tuning," but California interpretation. Studio: **Analog Machine Works**.

| We are | We are not |
|----|-------|
| Real driving in the mountains | Track day monster |
| Lightweight analog GT | Stance / show car |
| Weekend driver's car | Museum queen |
| OEM+ Motorsport | Drift / bro-tuning |

---

## Image

- Light, responsive, mechanical
- Sound, weight, balance, stance — not dyno numbers
- Visually and **tactilely** 10× donor price
- Same category as analog taste objects — Omega, Leica, Technics, Pelikan: **pleasure from use**, not specs on a screen
- **Phoebe Philo / Old Céline energy** — quiet luxury, architectural, material-led; insider taste, not logo flex
- Photo/video on real canyon roads — not parking lot
