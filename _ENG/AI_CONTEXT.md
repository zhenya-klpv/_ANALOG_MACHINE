## 2. Product philosophy

One phrase for every decision:

> **A lightweight, analog BMW reimagined as a modern Motorsport GT.**

If a part doesn't make the car **lighter, more beautiful, more reliable, or better to drive** — it isn't needed.

**We don't do:**
- widebody for widebody's sake
- LS swap / illegal swaps
- carbon show-off (eBay look)
- M3 CSL replica / M3 clone
- stance / drift aesthetic
- "the fastest E36" — we build **the most coherent**

**We do:**
- carbon panels, often **painted to body color**
- OEM+ Motorsport minimalism
- sharp suspension, brakes, alignment
- premium interior (Alcantara/leather, lightweight door cards)
- rebuilt M52/S52, 250–320 hp, reliability
- full build documentation (weight, hours, materials, CAD, tests)

## 4. Business model

### Now — Bootstrap (see [`BOOTSTRAP.md`](BOOTSTRAP.md))

| Phase | Timeline | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **1 Portfolio** | 0–3 mo | 1 part on E36 + content. Minimum cash out |
| **2 Paid micro** | 3–6 mo | $300–3k orders. Materials with client prepayment |
| **3 Deposits** | 6–12 mo | 50–70% deposit, 2–3 SKUs |
| **4 Workshop** | when rent covered | Rent only from work |

**Financial rule:** don't take new risk with loans + rent. Don't buy materials on your own money without a paid order.

### Micro-services (now)

$300–3k: carbon part, repair, trim panel, small aero, detailing+docs.

### Later — Full tiers (see [`PRICING.md`](PRICING.md))

Packages $5k–180k · Complete Commission from $120k · 12 numbered **Analog Machine** builds — **only after cash flow + proof**.

### Discipline
- 1 platform (E36)
- Deposit before work
- Labor hours tracked strictly
- Documentation — `DOCUMENTATION.md`
