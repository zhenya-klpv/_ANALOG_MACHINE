## 2. Product philosophy

One phrase for every decision:

> **A lightweight, analog BMW reimagined as a modern Motorsport GT.**

If a part doesn't make the car **lighter, more beautiful, more reliable, or better to drive** — it isn't needed.

**We don't do:**
- widebody for widebody's sake
- LS swap / illegal swaps
- carbon show-off (eBay look)
- M3 CSL replica / M3 clone
- stance / drift aesthetic
- "the fastest E36" — we build **the most coherent**

**We do:**
- carbon panels, often **painted to body color**
- OEM+ Motorsport minimalism
- sharp suspension, brakes, alignment
- premium interior (Alcantara/leather, lightweight door cards)
- rebuilt M52/S52, 250–320 hp, reliability
- full build documentation (weight, hours, materials, CAD, tests)

## 10. `_audi_s6` — trial (active)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Platform** | Audi S6 **C5** (A6/S6/RS6) · black interior |
| **Goal** | Relearn carbon; **molded parts**, not skinning |
| **Start** | `vent-main` — rectangular vent trim frame |
| **Approach** | OEM donor → **female mold** → carbon part; hybrid back (clips / 3D-print) |
| **Finish** | 2x2 twill 200 g/m² · epoxy · vacuum bag · **satin** 2K clear |
| **Do not touch** | vanes, slider, forged carbon, carbon wrap |

### Mold workflow (actual — per FGS order)

**Mold:** polyester Orange Tooling Gelcoat + MEKP → iso tooling resin + **1.5 oz chop mat** (3 yd) + **6 oz cloth** (3 yd).  
**Part:** West System **105 + 206** + own carbon cloth. Before each pull: wax + PVA on mold face.

*Eco choices:* no 10 oz cloth (#7 → extra mat layers); no peel ply (#19) yet; **403** instead of cotton flock (#9); **IPA 99%** instead of denatured alcohol.

### On hand (not purchased)
Vacuum film · carbon cloth · tape/fittings/hoses · brushes · nitrile gloves · scale 0.1 g · paper mix cups · **donor C5 vent frame** (master) · **IPA 99%**
