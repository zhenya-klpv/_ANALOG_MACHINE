## Product line (operational)

| Product | Real price | What's included (guide) |
|---------|---------------|------------------------|
| **Carbon / body package** | **$5k–20k** | Hood, trunk, splitter/diffuser, undertray, fenders (as ready), paint prep/fitment |
| **Suspension / brake / chassis package** | **$15k–35k** | Coilovers, bushings, arms, subframe refresh, brakes, lines, alignment, wheels/tires (optional) |
| **Interior package** | **$10k–30k** | Seats, door cards (composite), trim, steering wheel, hidden audio/CarPlay, wiring cleanup |
| **Engine / drivetrain package** | **$20k–60k+** | Rebuild M52/S52, cooling, exhaust (CARB-legal), LSD, trans refresh, engine bay detail |
| **Full E36 build** | **$90k–180k** | All packages + integration, paint, QC, documentation, build book |

**Lower bound of packages separately:** ~$50k (no donor, no paint, no integration).  
**Full build** — not just sum of packages: includes project management, fitment, paint, testing, margin.

## What affects position in range

| Factor | Lower ($) | Higher ($) |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Carbon | Hood + trunk only | Full aero, fenders, painted fitment |
| Chassis | Refresh + mid coilovers | Öhlins/MCS, BBK, forged wheels |
| Interior | Door cards + retrim | Custom seats, full rewire, premium audio |
| Engine | Refresh, stock power | Full rebuild, head work, dyno tune |
| Full build | Clean donor, minimal bodywork | Paint, structural repair, max spec |
