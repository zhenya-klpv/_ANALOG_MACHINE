## Technical approach

**Master → female mold → carbon part** (not skinning).  
**Mold:** polyester gelcoat + iso resin (FGS #16141). **Part:** West 105/206 epoxy + carbon.  
Hybrid: carbon face, OEM clips on back. See [`APPROACH.md`](APPROACH.md) · [`PROCESS.md`](PROCESS.md).
