# Market — Analog Machine

> Restomod market exists and pays. Question isn't "do people pay for quality," but **"do they pay for BMW E36 from a new brand."** Answer: **not immediately — prove first.**

---

## Market comparison

| Brand / product | Price | Notes |
|-----------------|------|---------|
| **Kindred** (electric classics) | $159k–$225k+ | Electric restomod, established brand |
| **Electric 911 restomod** | from $299k | Limited: 12 cars |
| **ECD Mustang** (Road & Track) | base ~$280k, build ~$362k | Premium restomod, high operational scale |
| **Singer / Theon** | $585k–$1.5M+ | Porsche, "definitive version" positioning |
| **Analog Machine — Full E36** | $90k–180k | Below luxury restomod tier, above BMW tuning |
| **Analog Machine — packages** | $5k–60k+ | Entry via parts/packages |

**Conclusion:** people pay for quality restomods. Our price — **accessible luxury** in the segment, not competing with Singer/ECD on ticket size.

## Realism (honest assessment)

| Model | Odds | Why |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Full custom car company immediately | **20–30%** | Capital, timelines, trust, ops risk |
| Premium composite + BMW package shop | **60–70%** | Discipline, repeatability, package cash flow |
| Hero car + site + documentation + 3–5 clients | **very realistic** | Right entry point |

**Strategy:** not "workshop for everyone," but **product company** with one platform.

## What kills the project

### 1. "We do everything for everyone"
E36 today, E39 tomorrow, Audi, Saab, Mustang — **death**.  
→ One platform. Then second — not in parallel.

### 2. Too much custom
Every "can we do something else?" eats margin.  
→ Standardize:

| Parameter | Limit (working) |
|----------|-----------------|
| Build levels | 3 |
| Colors | 12 |
| Interior options | 3 |
| Engines | 2 (both California-legal) |
| Philosophy | one — no exceptions at start |

### 3. No documentation
Without build book, car is "garage," not collectible.  
→ See [`DOCUMENTATION.md`](DOCUMENTATION.md)

### 4. Underestimating labor
Composite "we'll do it ourselves" → 80 hrs on master model, mold, fitment, paint, rework.  
→ **Count hours strictly** from day one. Cost sheet on every part.
