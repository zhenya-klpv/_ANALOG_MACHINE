# Vision — Analog Machine

## Philosophy

> **An analog Rolex, not an Apple Watch.**

> **Handbuilt in California**

> **Mechanical cars for the digital age.**

Analog cars built by hand in California — Highway 9, Skyline, Pacific Coast.  
Not an appliance, not a "tuning shop." Feel, presence, motion — not feeds.

## North star (long-term)

Create **Analog Machine** builds — **AM36**, **AM39**.  
Not "BMW restomod," but a **mechanical object** (like Alpina / Singer on their own platform).  
Built by **Analog Machine Works**, California.

## Now (bootstrap)

No capital → start with **composite craft + content**, not a coachbuilder shop.  
See [`BOOTSTRAP.md`](BOOTSTRAP.md). The dream stays alive — **deferred until financial safety**.

## Niche: California Canyon BMW

Not "BMW restomod" — **California canyon car**.

A lightweight analog car for Highway 9, Skyline, Santa Cruz Mountains.  
Not a track monster, not stance, not a museum queen — **a car for real driving**.

More detail: [`brand/POSITIONING.md`](brand/POSITIONING.md)

## Market position

| Singer / Theon | Analog Machine |
|----------------|----------------|
| Porsche 964, $585k–$1.5M+ | BMW E36 full build, $90k–180k |
| "Definitive 911" | "Definitive E36" — lightweight Motorsport GT |
| Carbon as luxury signal | Carbon as engineering + weight + form |
| Full commission from day one | Demo car → packages → commissions |

We don't compete with Singer on price. We compete on **coherence, process, and trust** in our niche.

## Visual language

- **OEM+ Motorsport minimalism** — like a factory prototype they never released
- Painted carbon, not exposed weave (except controlled accent details)
- Period-correct proportions, no stance, no gratuitous widebody
- Interior: Alcantara, leather, clean lines, hidden modern tech
- Photography: architectural, quiet luxury — not garage chaos

## Customer

Someone who wants **Singer vibe without Porsche money**.  
Values mechanics, weight, stance, build quality — not horsepower wars on YouTube.

## What we sell

Not just the car. We sell:

1. **Trust** — documentation, weights, hours, tests
2. **Material language** — our own composite parts, not catalog
3. **Coherence** — every part serves the whole
4. **California legality** — drivable, not hidden from smog
5. **Story** — beautifully shot process and video; brand visible before first client

See [`brand/CONTENT.md`](brand/CONTENT.md)

## Anti-patterns

- Another "custom E36" in the Instagram feed
- Project without standards — every build from scratch
- Promises to clients before demo car is ready
- Complexity for complexity's sake (roof carbon first, 600 hp, LS swap)

## Success looks like

Someone sees your E36 and says:

> **"I didn't think an E36 could look and feel this expensive."**

Not the most powerful. Not the most expensive. **The most coherent.**

## Product discipline

Standardize, don't customize:

- 3 build levels
- 12 colors
- 3 interior options
- 2 engines (California-legal)
- 1 platform (E36) until concept is proven

## Limited edition (later)

**12 numbered builds** · E36 · California-built · carbon composite · after hero car + 3–5 clients.
