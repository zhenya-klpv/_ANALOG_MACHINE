# Investors — Analog Machine

> **Now:** don't seek investors. **Create investable proof** in 1–3 months.  
> With loans + rent — no personal loan, no credit card, no shop lease on own money.

---

## Honestly: what's too early

Pitching "we'll lease a workshop, buy equipment, build restomods like Singer" — **doesn't work** without:

- clients and orders
- proof that car/part sells for $X
- answer to "why won't you burn on the first project"

Investor sees **expensive garage dream**, not brand.

---

## Goal now

Not "find an investor."  
**Create a reason** for an investor to understand what they're getting into.

### Proof in 1–3 months (without big money)

| # | Deliverable |
|---|-------------|
| 1 | Name + concept: Analog Machine · Handbuilt in California |
| 2 | Demo: AM36 teaser · 1 composite part · AM badge · plaque · process video |
| 3 | Instagram 10–20 quality posts · mini-site or landing |
| 4 | **3–5 paid interest** or first order with **prepayment** |

## Who to seek (later — not VC)

| Type | What they give | Range |
|-----|----------|----------|
| **Angel — car community** | Cash for proof | $10k–50k |
| **Strategic partner** | Shop, paint, CNC, upholstery, media — **better than cash** | Barter / revenue share |
| **Client-investor** | Deposit on build/part | Ideal "investor" = demand |

**Not a fit:** classic VC (not software).

## China — partners, not "investor for the dream"

> Not "Chinese investor for restomod." But **partner / patron / sourcing** for California handbuilt brand.

### Why it might work

**Handbuilt in California** — strong signal for collectors in China: Western craft, status, scarcity, lifestyle.

Need: visual brand, story, Instagram/YouTube + **小红书 / Douyin / Bilibili** (later).

### What they'll ask

Not "give us money for shop." But: equity? distribution? first car? exclusivity? production? **IP?**

### Realistic formats

| Format | Description |
|--------|----------|
| **Client-patron** | Deposit on AM36 No. 001 / Founding Commission — best option |
| **Production partner** | Molds, CNC, carbon fabric, badges, packaging — **careful with IP** |
| **Media / distribution** | Partner for China car culture — may be more useful than cash |
| **Diaspora in California** | Chinese-American enthusiast Bay Area / LA / Irvine — **most realistic path** |

Search not "in China," but **Chinese-speaking car/business partner in California** with capital + audience + sourcing + **possibly shop access**.

### Pitch (short)

> Analog Machine builds handbuilt mechanical cars in California for collectors who still want feel, craft, and analog driving. First platform: BMW E36/E39, composite bodywork, AM identity.

> *A California handbuilt analog car brand for the post-EV world.*

### Ask (specific)

- $15k–30k for demo + molds, **or**
- shop/resource partnership, **or**
- first commission deposit

**Roles:** Founding partner · first patron · China-facing audience partner.

### Pitch deck (5–7 pages, when proof exists)

1. Brand — Analog Machine · Handbuilt in California  
2. Why now — post-EV, screens → mechanical feel  
3. First product — AM36  
4. What we make — composites now, builds later  
5. Proof — composites skill, E36, content  
6. Ask — see above  
7. Partner role — patron / space / network  

### Risks — don't give away early

- ❌ All CAD / models without NDA
- ❌ Full part design
- ❌ China exclusivity "on a handshake"
- ❌ Equity without lawyer
- ❌ Production without QC
- ❌ Money without contract

Bad first batch = death of premium reputation.

### "Space + China" combo

Ideal partner gives not only capital but **workshop access** — see [`WORKSPACE.md`](WORKSPACE.md).

**Not:** "we need workshop lease."  
**Yes:** "we need founding partner with space or patron for AM36 No. 001."
