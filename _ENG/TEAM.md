# Founding Team — Analog Machine

> **Not solo** — small team with the right roles.  
> Full-time jobs for everyone → **nights-only California handbuilt project**, not panic shop.

---

## Team (3–4)

**Minimum to start: 3.** **Maximum at bootstrap: 4.** Five or more — too many for nights-only; slows decisions and dilutes equity.

### Core (3) — required

| Person | Role | Responsibility |
|---------|------|----------------------|
| **Zhenya** | Creative Director · Brand · Build Lead | Concept, visual, Instagram/YouTube, part design, content, QC, clients/partners |
| **Friend with E36** | Vehicle Lead · Mechanical Lead | Donor car, mechanics, suspension/brakes/drivetrain, teardown/assembly, OEM+ plan |
| **Composite engineer** | Composite Lead · Product Lead | Materials, molds, technology, repeatability, tests, product parts |

### Ideal 4th — **with premises**

**Best-case fourth founder:** someone who brings **workspace** — garage, barn, shop corner, rural property. Solves the hardest bootstrap problem (where to work) without $300–700/mo Craigslist hunt.

| They contribute | Team gets |
|-----------------|-----------|
| 2-car garage / barn / shared bay | Evening access, composite + film, one E36 |
| Electricity, dry roof, lockable space | No commercial lease drama |
| Local knowledge (Highway 9, South Bay) | Content atmosphere + logistics |

**Equity:** space is a **real contribution** — larger share or reduced rent / barter — but **in writing** (see `FOUNDER_AGREEMENT.md`, Option C logic). Not "free garage = automatic 25%."

### Other 4th roles — if no premises candidate

Add a fourth **only** if they close a gap the core three can't cover. Not "a friend who wants in."

| Possible 4th role | When it makes sense |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Premises + light fab** | Garage owner who also welds, trims, or preps body |
| **Film / content lead** | Zhenya can't carry shoot + edit + brand alone |
| **Paint / body** | Real paint program in their space |
| **CAD / fabrication** | Metal, jigs, trim beyond composite scope |

**Rule:** 4th joins with **written role + hours/week + what they contribute (space? tools?) + vesting** — same as everyone else. No "advisor with 25%."

See [`WORKSPACE.md`](WORKSPACE.md) · if 4th has space, Craigslist becomes plan B.

## Main danger

Not money. Not garage. **Blurred expectations between friends.**

Talk through **before** money, garage, Instagram:

- [ ] Whose E36 after the build?
- [ ] Who owns the Analog Machine brand?
- [ ] How do we split money?
- [ ] Who pays for materials?
- [ ] Who makes final decisions?
- [ ] If someone leaves — what happens?
- [ ] Right to shoot/show car for brand?
- [ ] Who owns part designs?
- [ ] Can parts be sold separately?

## Shares and equity

**Not equal split by headcount** (33% × 3 or 25% × 4) — contributions differ.

| Principle | Rule |
|---------|---------|
| Ownership | Car, tools — belong to owners |
| Brand | Co-founders per agreement |
| Future equity | Vesting 6–12 mo by contribution/time |
| Early exit | Doesn't take a proportional chunk of the brand without vesting |
| Money/car | Separate contribution, not automatic equity |

LLC — later. Now: **Founder Agreement 1–2 pages.**
