## Quick pick (2026)

| Need | Where | Why |
|------|-------|-----|
| **First mold kit all-in-one** | [Fiberglass Supply](https://fiberglasssupply.com) | Single cart: wax, PVA, gelcoat, iso, West System, mat |
| **Today / no shipping** | **TAP Plastics San Jose** | PP sheet, wax, cloth, epoxy, flange |
| **Epoxy tooling mold** | [Fibre Glast](https://www.fibreglast.com) · [Rock West](https://rockwestcomposites.com) | Epoxy surface coat, best range for carbon molds |
| **Carbon 2x2 twill small yd** | [Composite Envisions](https://www.compositeenvisions.com) | From ~$4/yd, 1 yd+, qty discounts |
| **Prepreg (not bootstrap)** | [Rock West prepregs](https://www.rockwestcomposites.com/prepregs) | Retail; needs freezer + oven/autoclave |
| **West System refill** | West Marine · TAP · FGS | Compare price + shipping |
| **2K satin clear (#17)** | Paint supply / TCP Global | Not composites shop |

**FGS ≠ best everywhere.** Best = depends on category and urgency. See [`MATRIX.md`](MATRIX.md).
