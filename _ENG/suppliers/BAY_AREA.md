### What to buy locally

| Category | Items |
|----------|-------|
| **Mold setup** | PP sheet, HDPE, corrugated sign board, clay |
| **Polyester mold** | Gelcoat, MEKP, iso/poly resin, mat, cloth |
| **Epoxy** | TAP 314 system (compare with West System) |
| **Carbon** | Carbon cloth section — check 2x2 twill stock |
| **Tools** | Rollers, brushes, mixing cups, gloves |
| **Plastics** | Lexan, acrylic — flanges, templates |

### When to go to TAP instead of FGS

- #11 PP sheet
- Need 1 yd mat/cloth
- Need wax/PVA today
- Save **$60–90 shipping**

## Not local (mail-order)

- Orange **tooling** gelcoat (sometimes TAP generic gelcoat — not the same)
- Iso **tooling** resin gallon (TAP may have laminating resin — check spec)
- Specialty prepregs
- Partall wax/PVA (sometimes TAP; else FGS/Fibre Glast)

## San Jose procurement route

```
Urgent / small / PP     → TAP Alameda (~15 min)
West System refill      → West Marine nearest
Satin clear + sandpaper → paint supply
Big mold kit            → FGS or Fibre Glast (ship)
Carbon 5+ yd best price → Composite Envisions (ship)
```
